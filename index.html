Promptly Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the Promptly app for Chrome, Firefox and any other platforms.

Information we collect

We store data to save LLM prompts that users have submitted. This saved data is used to improve future results from our search engine for all users. Users can enter a satisfaction score and additional feedback for a prompt, which is stored anonymously in our database. We do not collect any data from users who do not submit feedback. Users do not create an account, and we do not store email addresses or passwords.

Technical basics

GitHub Pages: Our website is hosted using GitHub Pages. GitLab's privacy statement can be found at https://about.gitlab.com/privacy/.

Google Sheets: We use Google Sheets to store our database of user-submitted prompts. Google's Privacy Policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Heroku: We use Heroku to host our server. Heroku's Privacy Policy can be found at https://www.heroku.com/policy/security.

Information usage: We use the information we collect to operate our apps.

We do not share personal information with outside parties.

We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property.

In the future, we may sell to, buy, merge with, or partner with other businesses. In such transactions, user information may be among the transferred assets.

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance: We never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our app is structured to attract anyone under 13.

Your Consent: By using our app, you consent to our privacy policy.

Contacting Us: If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, you may email promptly.extension@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy: If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. Summary of changes so far:
